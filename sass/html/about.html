work about contact

Allen Hu is a designer based in New York City. He is interested in design because it can communicate personality through color, and form. Design can exist in various ways like pattern-design, illustrations, photography, and typography. He believes that design should be a holistic approach rather than living as just a typeface or poster.

He dabbles in photography and type-design in his spare time. You can find him exploring the world through @allenhu_designs on instagram.
