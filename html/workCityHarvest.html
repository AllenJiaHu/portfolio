Re-branding City Harvest

Branding

Illustration

City Harvest is a nonprofit that values sustainable practices in food waste. Re-branding was needed because the previous identity system lacked consistency in its language and visual motifs.

The logo concept that I contributed uses the motif of a building holding groceries. The logo represents their mission, which is to reduce food waste by taking leftover food from restaurants and redistributing it to shelters.
