MoMA Exhibition: Autobodies Poster

poster design

This is a hypothetical poster designed for MoMA's car exhibition. My design idea was to create abstracted car illustrations and arrange them in a way to appear as if they were moving across the composition. I intentionally abstracted the cars in order to not reveal too much information.
